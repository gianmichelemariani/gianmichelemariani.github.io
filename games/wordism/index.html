Wordism

Wordism is a fun, new and relaxing word game that is going to light up your inner passion for words.
Challenge yourself with six different play modes ranging from word searches to time challenges and infinite combo tricks.
Discover how to get more points with unusual combinations, climb the leaderboards and complete the crazy 100 questions challenge.
New modes are already in the work and will be freely available.

  • Completely free. No Ads, no tricks, no tracking.
  • Beautiful minimalistic design with typography at its core.
  • Daily word search for a relaxing few minutes.
  • Infinity mode with combo sessions.
  • Meteor mode with falling and burning letters.
  • Answer 100 questions from Quiz mode
  • Challenge yourself with a quick 3 minutes word hunt.
  • Try to stop the ever growing Tower of letters.
