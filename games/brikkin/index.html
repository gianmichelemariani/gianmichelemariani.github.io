Brikkin

Brikkin is a relaxing puzzle game to master your inner balance. Slide all the brikks along the grid and match the original design.
Play against time and your perception while enjoying the beautiful soundtrack.

  • 50 beautifully designed levels.
  • Relaxing gameplay with a little challenge.
  • Secrets to discover.
  • 12 achievements.
  • Transporting soundtrack by Blue Dot Sessions.
